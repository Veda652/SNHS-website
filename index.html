About Us

The SNHS chapter at John Jay fosters academic excellence, leadership, and service in science.

Membership Requirements

  • Minimum 3.5 GPA in science courses
  • Enrolled in an Honors/AP science class
  • 10+ science-related service hours per semester
  • Consistent attendance and participation
  • Exemplary character and academic integrity

President

Veda Kodakandla

Veda is a sophomore in John Jay Senior High School, and is proud to be the president and founder of this chapter of the Science National Honor Society. Her passions include playing the violin, singing traditional Carnatic music, competing in chess tournaments, and battling through rigorous Model UN debates. Shes hopes to become an oncologist, majoring in biochemistry and biology.
