About Our Chapter

The John Jay Senior High School Science National Honor Society (SNHS) chapter is a passionate community of students dedicated to the pursuit of scientific knowledge, service, and scholarship. Our mission is to foster a love for science while giving back to the school and local community through tutoring, STEM events, and scientific outreach.

Her passions include playing the violin, singing traditional Carnatic music, competing in chess tournaments, and battling through rigorous Model UN debates.
